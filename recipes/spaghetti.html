Spaghetti

Picture by Alice Ponce

Description

Spaghetti is a classic that everyone knows. This recipe will be for store bought basic spaghetti with meat. Not much else to say about spaghetti.

Ingredients

  • 6-8 Cups of Water
  • 16oz of Dried Pasta
  • 24oz of Spaghetti Sauce
  • 1 Lb of Ground Beef
  • Optional: 2 Tablespoons of Olive Oil
  • Optional: Preferred Seasonings

Steps

  1. Bring 6-8 cups of water to a roaring boil
    1. While waiting for water to boil, brown meat on medium high.
    2. Add desired seasonings to meat.
    3. Drain grease from pan and add sauce to meat.
    4. Add desired seasonings to the sauce.
    5. Heat sauce on medium low while occasional stirring until it starts to bubble. Keep Covered.
    6. Remove from heat and uncover.
  2. Add pasta and olive oil to water.
  3. Cook for 4-10 minutes depending on type of pasta you have and desired tenderness. (6 minutes is good for most types).
  4. Pour pasta in strainer to remove water.
  5. Add sauce either in the pot or on top of pasta in bowl and enjoy!
