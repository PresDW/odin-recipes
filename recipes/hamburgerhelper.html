Hamburger Helper

Description

Cheeseburger Hamburger Helper is as simple as it gets! Browned ground beef with some milk and special cheese sauce done in 20 minutes. Feeds 2-4 people and is one of my personal favorites. All you need in your kitchen is a stove and a medium sized pan.

Ingredients

  • Hamburger Helper Box Containing:
    • Dried Pasta
    • Sauce Packet
  • 2 Cups of 2% Milk
  • 1 Lb of Ground Beef
  • 1 and 1/3 Cups of Water

Steps

  1. Brown 1 Lb of ground beef on medium high heat, drain pan once browned.
  2. Stir in water, milk, sauce packet and dried pasta.
  3. Heat to boiling
  4. Once boiling reduce heat, simmer for 12 minutes while stirring occasionally.
  5. Uncover and cook for 1-3 minutes while stirring until you reach a desired consistency.
