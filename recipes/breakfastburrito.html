Breakfast Burrito

Picture by Victoria.

Description

A Breakfast Burrito is an excellent choice for meal prepping or a simple but delicious option for a weekend treat. Easily customizable and quick to make and with ingredients that wont break the bank it's my favorite breakfast food. The recipe being used here will be using bacon but any meat or no meat works just as well!

Ingredients

  • Eggs(1-2 per burrito).
  • Your preferred shredded cheese.
  • Burrito sized soft tortillas.
  • Hashbrown patties
  • Optional: Bacon, Sausage, Ham, Etc.

Steps

  1. Complete all cooking steps simultaneously when possible to allow the food to be as hot as possible.
  2. Optional: Cook your preferred meat on stove top.
    1. For bacon place on cold pan and cook on medium low heat until bacon starts to curl.
    2. Flip bacon and continue flipping to ensure even cooking until it reaches desired crispiness.
    3. Drain pan of grease and place bacon to the side.
  3. Cook hashbrowns in air fryer or oven according to specific brand instructions.
  4. Scramble 1-2 eggs per burrito you plan to make. Place aside until final step
  5. Place tortilla on plate and put 1-2 eggs worth of scrambled eggs in center of tortilla
  6. Place 1 hashbrown in center on tortilla.
  7. Optional: Place meat in center of tortilla. For bacon 2-3 strips.
  8. Place half a cup of shredded cheese into center of tortilla.
  9. Take the 2 long sides of the burrito (where the hashbrown's long ends are facing) and pull to center of tortilla.
  10. Roll the short side over the center to encapsulate all the food and going over the tortilla you pulled to the center in step 9 within the burrito.
  11. Enjoy!
